Spaghetti Recipe

How to Make Spaghetti



Quick and easy Spaghetti Marinara from allrecipes.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 (28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14.5 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp. minced garlic
  • 2 tsp. white sugar
  • 2 tsp. dried parsley
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp. dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. capers
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes (optional)



Steps


  1. Step 1

    2. In a large saucepan combine crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, minced garlic, sugar, parsley, garlic powder, salt, oregano, basil, and ground black pepper. Add capers and crushed red pepper if desired. Cover. Bring to a boil.

  2. Step 2

    3. Lower heat and simmer, with cover, for 45 to 60 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. As simmering time nears, in a large pot with boiling salted water cook spaghetti until al dente.

  4. Step 4

    5. Toss spaghetti with cooked sauce. Serve warm.
